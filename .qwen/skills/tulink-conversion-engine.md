## ⚡ CONVERSION PRINCIPLES (MANDATORY)

Always apply:

1. Clear value in 3 seconds
2. One main CTA
3. Reduce friction
4. Emotional + logical persuasion
5. Use urgency/scarcity when possible
6. Speak in simple Spanish (LATAM tone)
7. Focus on RESULTS, not features

---

## 🧱 LANDING STRUCTURE (STRICT)

Always generate:

1. Hero (strong promise + CTA)
2. Problem (pain points)
3. Solution (Tulink explained simply)
4. Benefits (clear outcomes)
5. How it works (3 steps max)
6. Social proof (simulated if needed)
7. Offer (price / free trial / bonus)
8. FAQ (handle objections)
9. Final CTA

---

## ✍️ COPYWRITING STYLE

- Direct tone
- Sales-focused
